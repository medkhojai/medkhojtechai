About MedKhoj

MedKhoj is an AI-powered research platform developed to accelerate pharmaceutical innovation, particularly in the area of drug repurposing.

Our mission is to leverage artificial intelligence to help researchers identify new therapeutic applications for existing drugs, reducing development time and costs while bringing treatments to patients faster.

Founded as part of EY's Techathon initiative, MedKhoj combines domain expertise in pharmaceuticals with cutting-edge AI technology to deliver actionable insights.
